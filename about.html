About KNOTT

Born from a belief
that connection is everything.

We are a Toronto-based Customer Journey Design studio. We help brands create the moments their clients, employees, and partners carry with them long after the event ends.

Our Story

How KNOTT
came to be.

KNOTT was founded in Toronto in 2025 on a simple but powerful idea — that the way a brand makes people feel is its most valuable asset. Not the logo. Not the tagline. The feeling.

We started with objects. Precision-designed tools that bring focus, calm, and intention to the everyday. Then we realized the same philosophy applied to experiences — that a corporate event, a curated gift, a thoughtful moment — could be just as deliberately designed as any object.

Today KNOTT sits at the intersection of design, experience, and brand strategy. We help companies create the moments their people remember — because we believe the most powerful marketing is the one people feel.

The Founders

Two minds.
One vision.

Mandana and Catherine met during their MBA in Toronto in 2024. They shared the same frustration — most brands invest in what they say, not in how they make people feel. KNOTT exists to change that.

What We Stand For

Three beliefs that
drive everything we do.

01

Feeling over function

Every object, every event, every moment we design starts with one question — how should this feel? Function follows. Feeling leads.

02

Intention in everything

Nothing we do is generic. Every gift is chosen. Every experience is designed. The moment it becomes templated, it stops being KNOTT.

03

Objects worth keeping

We only put our name on things we would keep ourselves. If it ends up in a drawer, we've failed. If it stays on someone's desk — that's KNOTT.
