About KNOTT

Born from a belief
that connection is everything.

A Toronto-based studio designing the moments that make brands worth remembering.

What is KNOTT

We design how
your brand is felt.

KNOTT is a Customer Journey Design studio based in Toronto. We help companies create meaningful moments — the events, the gifts, the experiences — that shape how people feel about a brand long after the moment ends.

We sit at the intersection of design, experience, and brand strategy. Our work is for companies that believe the way they show up for their clients, teams, and partners is not a detail — it is the whole point.

From a brand activation in a Porsche pit to a curated desk object that lives on someone's table for years — every KNOTT project starts with one question: how should this feel?

Why a Knot?
Because connection holds.

A knot is the oldest symbol of connection — two threads pulled together into something that holds. Simple. Intentional. Human.

In a world of AI, hybrid work, and digital everything, the warmth between people fades quickly. A brand that invests in how it makes people feel is rare. We exist to help those brands create moments that hold — long after the meeting ends, the event closes, the gift is opened.

A KNOTT is not decoration. It is the thing that keeps two threads together.

Mandana & Catherine · Toronto, 2024

Two threads.
One KNOTT.

We met during our MBA in Toronto in 2024 and discovered we shared the same frustration — most brands invest in what they say, not in how they make people feel. We knew there was a better way to build real connections between companies and the people who matter most to them.

Mandana is an architect by training with a background in design and entrepreneurship. She believes every object and experience should earn its place in someone's life — and that if something doesn't feel considered, it shouldn't exist. She brings the creative vision, the product instinct, and the eye for what makes a moment beautiful.

Catherine brings over a decade of corporate experience and a deep understanding of what makes relationships between companies and people last. She translates KNOTT's philosophy into experiences that actually work for real businesses — and she asks one question about everything we do: will people remember this?

Together, they are what a knot is. Two different threads pulled into something that holds. Imagination and execution. Design and strategy. The creative and the commercial.

What We Stand For

Three beliefs that
drive everything we do.

01

Feeling over function

Every object, every event, every moment starts with one question — how should this feel? Function follows. Feeling leads.

02

Intention in everything

Nothing we do is generic. Every gift is chosen. Every experience is designed. The moment it becomes templated, it stops being KNOTT.

03

Objects worth keeping

We only put our name on things we would keep ourselves. If it ends up in a drawer, we've failed. If it stays on someone's desk for years — that's KNOTT.

"We didn't start KNOTT to build a company. We started it because we genuinely believe that the moments between people — the events, the gifts, the experiences — are the most important investments a brand can make."

— Mandana & Catherine, Co-founders
