About KNOTT

Born from a belief that connection is everything.

A Toronto-based studio designing the moments that make brands worth remembering.

What is KNOTT

KNOTT is a Customer Journey Design studio. We help companies create the events, the gifts, and the experiences that shape how people feel about a brand long after the moment ends — because how you show up for clients, teams, and partners is the whole point, not a detail.

Mandana & Catherine · Toronto, 2024

Two threads.
One KNOTT.

We met during our MBA in Toronto in 2024 and discovered we shared the same frustration — most brands invest in what they say, not in how they make people feel.

A knot is the oldest symbol of connection: two threads pulled together into something that holds. Simple. Intentional. Human. That's what we build into every event, gift, and object we put our name on.

Mandana is an architect by training, with a background in design and entrepreneurship. She believes every object and experience should earn its place in someone's life — bringing the creative vision, the product instinct, and the eye for what makes a moment beautiful.

Catherine brings over a decade of corporate experience and a deep understanding of what makes relationships between companies and people last. She asks one question about everything we do: will people remember this?

Together, they are what a knot is — two different threads pulled into something that holds. Imagination and execution. Design and strategy. The creative and the commercial.
